Premium products, medical grade About Cannetics

Cannetics is located on Sjælland and will start its growing business in Q4 2018 and expects to release first badge in the spring of 2019.

Product will be sold to Danish manufacturers and as export goods if regulations in Jan 2019 see changes accordingly.

Mission
Cannetics is a new company which is targetting the cannabis cultivation market in Denmark, Sca and Northern Europe. Cannetics is applying for a cannabis bulk cultivating license and will initially establish one indoor cultivation facility at 10,000 sq. ft. on a confidential location on Sjælland. With strong partnerships and connection to the leading research fields of medical cannabis and extensive knowledge from the industry, we will bring high-potent premium medical grade cannabis flowers to whole buyers around Europe.

Products & Services
Cannetics will produce up to 6 strains within 3 months of becoming a licensed cultivator. We will have the ability to vegetate up to 1000 plants and flowers concurrently. Cannetics will grow up to 20 different strains of marijuana, and will have the ability to grow from seedling to finished product. Cannetics will dry and process all cannabis flowers into labeled/inventoried vacuum sealed bags before delivery to manufacturers.
